Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

1. Introduction

At FocusFlow, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

Key Point: FocusFlow stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data.

2. Data Storage

FocusFlow uses your browser's localStorage to save all your data. This means:

  • Your tasks, notes, habits, and goals are stored only on your device
  • We cannot see, access, or delete your data
  • Your data is not transmitted to any external servers
  • Data is not backed up by us - you are responsible for your own backups

3. Information We Do Not Collect

FocusFlow does NOT collect:

  • Your email address or personal information
  • Your tasks, notes, or any content you create
  • Your browsing history
  • Your location data
  • Device information or analytics

4. Local Storage

We store the following data locally on your device:

  • User profile information (email, name, plan type)
  • Tasks and subtasks
  • Notes
  • Habits and habit completion history
  • Goals and progress
  • Pomodoro session history
  • Application settings and preferences

5. Third-Party Services

FocusFlow does not share your data with any third-party services. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising networks that would collect information about your usage.

6. Data Security

While we do not have access to your data, we recommend:

  • Not clearing your browser's localStorage if you want to keep your data
  • Exporting your data regularly if you need a backup
  • Using a private browsing mode only when you want to start fresh

7. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally, you have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete your data by clearing browser localStorage
  • You can export your data by accessing localStorage directly
  • You can stop using the app at any time without consequences

8. Children's Privacy

FocusFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@focusflow.app
